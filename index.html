Neurology

The Neurology department specializes in the study and treatment of disorders affecting the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Our curriculum integrates clinical practice with cutting-edge research, allowing students to explore various neurological conditions. We aim to equip future neurologists with the skills needed to improve patient outcomes and enhance quality of life.

Hepatology

Our Hepatology division is dedicated to the study of liver diseases, including hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. Students receive comprehensive training in diagnosing and managing liver conditions, with opportunities for hands-on experience in specialized clinics. We emphasize the importance of research in understanding liver health and developing innovative treatment strategies.

Pediatrics

The Pediatrics department is committed to the health and well-being of infants, children, and adolescents. Our program provides students with a robust foundation in pediatric medicine, focusing on growth, development, and preventive care. Through clinical rotations and community engagement, students learn to address the unique healthcare needs of younger populations.

Eye Care

Our Eye Care department offers a comprehensive approach to the diagnosis and treatment of ocular conditions, including refractive errors, glaucoma, and retinal diseases. Students are trained in both clinical and surgical aspects of ophthalmology, gaining valuable experience in patient care. We are dedicated to advancing eye health through research, education, and community outreach initiatives.
